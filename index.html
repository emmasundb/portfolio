Design Portfolio Guidlines, 10/16/2024

A design portfolio is a book or website in which an engineer, designer, or computer scientist will formally document, in chronological order, all of their work that is associated with a specific design project.

It's important to keep a design portfolio because it helps keep your work organized and allows you to prove it as your own. Additionally, it is typically required by companies and is recognized as a legal document in patent activities. It proves the origin of an idea, your diligence in turning an idea into a solution, and when an idea became a working solution.
